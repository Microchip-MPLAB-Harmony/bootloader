Hardware Setup
  1. Project sam_d21_xpro.X
    • Hardware Used
      • SAM D21 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
      • IO1 Xplained Pro Extension Kit
    • Hardware Setup
      • Connect IO1 Xplained Pro Extension Kit to the EXT1 header of SAM D21 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
      • Insert the micro-SD Card in the micro-SD Card slot of the IO1 Xplained Pro Extension Kit
      • Connect the I2C SDA line of the I2C slave to Pin 11 (SERCOM2 PAD[0]/PA08) on EXT2 of SAM D21 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
      • Connect the I2C SCL line of the I2C slave to Pin 12 (SERCOM2 PAD[1]/PA09) on EXT2 of SAM D21 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
      • Connect a ground wire between the SAM D21 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit and the I2C slave
      • Connect a micro USB cable to the DEBUG USB port

 

  1. Project sam_e54_xpro.X
    • Hardware Used
      • SAM E54 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
    • Hardware Setup
      • Insert the SD Card in the SD Card slot of the Device.
      • Connect the I2C SDA line of the I2C slave to Pin 11 (SERCOM7 PAD[0]/PD08) on EXT3 of SAM E54 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
      • Connect the I2C SCL line of the I2C slave to Pin 12 (SERCOM7 PAD[1]/PD09) on EXT3 of SAM E54 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit
      • Connect a ground wire between the SAM E54 Xplained Pro Evaluation Kit and the I2C slave
      • Connect a micro USB cable to the DEBUG USB port
